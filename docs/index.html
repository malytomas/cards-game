Preparation

Players choose their favorite race and reveal them simultaneously. Use the Race cards.

  • Players have chosen different races: deal all cards of the corresponding races to the players.

  • Players have chosen same race: take all cards of that race, shuffle them, and deal half to each player. In case of odd number of cards, the last one is discarded.

The Race cards are discarded.

Repeat the above process one additional time.

Afterwards, shuffle all remaining cards and deal to players until they have 50.

Players who have more than 50 cards will discard them randomly.

Each player may discard up to 10 cards and draw again same amount.

Finally, each player receives 3 universal resource tokens (can be used as any of / / ).

Turns

The game is played in 5 turns.

First player goes through all phases described below, in this order. After that goes the second player.

Production phase

Put extra cards face-down on creatures or buildings as the tokens they have produced.

The kind of a token is always know by the card it is placed on.

Tokens are always face-down cards taken from the deck that is not assigned to either player when preparing the game.

You may choose from which cards to take the tokens when spending them. It is possible to spend tokens from multiple cards at once.

Combat phase

Combat is optional.

Attacking will prevent you from finishing remaining phases in this turn (you will be unable to place new creature into the game).

Winner of the combat kills (disables) one of opponent's creatures or buildings.

State your intention if you have decided to attack, and proceed by the combat rules.

Creatures phase

Place one creature or building into the play on your side of the table.

If the creature has effects, they are immediately applied, except for production.

Items phase

Give items to own creatures.

You may give up to 2 items in one turn.

Effects on the items, if any, are immediately applied, except for production.

Each creature may have at most one item. Buildings cannot have any items.

Spells phase

Play non-combat spells.

You may play up to 2 spells in one turn.

Used spell cards are removed from the game.

Final game evaluation

Dead creatures may be resurrected for 1 each and buildings for 1 each.

All at the end rules are applied.

Each creature and building decides if it is considered as attacking, defending, or out of combat.

All non-final additive, multiplicative, and conversion effects are applied, in this order.

Attributes from all cards are summed for each player.

All final effects are applied: additive, multiplicative, and conversions, in this order.

  • The player who leads (by any amount) in more attributes ( / / ) wins.
